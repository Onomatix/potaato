## Image Requirements
- Resolution: 400x300 pixels minimum
- Format: JPG
- Style: Top-down or 45-degree angle view
- Lighting: Well-lit, natural lighting preferred
- Background: Clean, simple background
- Content: Food should fill at least 80% of the frame
